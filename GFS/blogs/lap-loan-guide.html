Things You Need for LAP Loan

Simple information about loan against property documents, basics, and application tips.

Introduction

A Loan Against Property helps you raise funds by keeping your property as security. It can be useful for business needs, education, medical expenses, or other large goals.

Documents Required

You should usually keep these documents ready before applying:

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhar Card
  • Address Proof
  • Income Proof
  • Bank Statements
  • Property Papers
  • Passport Size Photo
  • ITR

Helpful Tips

Keep your property documents clear, maintain a good credit score, and check eligibility before applying.
